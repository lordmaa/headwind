Setup Guide
Strava Integration
  1. Go to strava.com/settings/api and create an app. Set the callback domain to your server's hostname or IP.
  2. Copy your Client ID and Client Secret into STRAVA_CLIENT_ID and STRAVA_CLIENT_SECRET.
  3. Visit /auth/strava to complete the OAuth handshake — once only.
Auto-Sync Webhook
  1. Set APP_URL to your public URL (e.g. https://headwind.yourdomain.com). A Cloudflare Tunnel works well.
  2. Set STRAVA_WEBHOOK_TOKEN to any secret string.
  3. Click Subscribe in the Webhook section above.
Home Assistant / MQTT
