Setup Guide
Strava Integration
  1. Go to strava.com/settings/api and create an app. Set the callback domain to your server's hostname or IP.
  2. Copy your Client ID and Client Secret into the STRAVA_CLIENT_ID and STRAVA_CLIENT_SECRET environment variables.
  3. Visit /auth/strava to complete the OAuth handshake — this only needs to be done once.
Auto-Sync Webhook

The webhook means new rides appear automatically the moment you finish — no manual sync needed. For it to work, Strava needs to reach your server from the internet.

  1. Set APP_URL to your publicly reachable URL (e.g. https://headwind.yourdomain.com). A Cloudflare Tunnel or reverse proxy works well.
  2. Set STRAVA_WEBHOOK_TOKEN to any secret string you choose.
  3. Click Subscribe in the Auto-Sync Webhook section above. Strava will verify the callback and activate it.

Home Assistant / MQTT

Enter your MQTT broker address, port, and credentials in the Home Assistant section above. Once saved, 17 sensors publish automatically every 20 seconds and after every ride sync. New ride notifications route to your HA companion app — set the HA Device field on each rider's profile page.

Garmin Connect

Enter your Garmin Connect email and password in the Garmin Connect section above. The first sync may require interactive login — run python3 garmin_setup.py inside the container if it fails. After that, recovery data (resting HR, HRV, sleep, body battery) syncs every few hours automatically.
