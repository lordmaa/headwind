How riders work
🔑 One Strava account per instance. The owner rider's rides sync automatically via Strava. That's you.
👥 Additional riders are co-riders — training partners, family, club mates. Their rides are imported manually via FIT or GPX file. They don't need a Strava account.
📊 Every rider gets their own stats, segment PRs, achievements, and AI coaching. Heatmap and segments are shared across everyone on the instance.
➕ You can add or remove riders any time from the Riders page.
